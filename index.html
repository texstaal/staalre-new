Why STAAL

New to the Dutch market, or growing in it? You shouldn’t have to navigate it alone. Local market knowledge, direct lines to owners, and one point of contact from first search to final signature.

More than square metres.

Your warehouse is your operation — inbound, storage, fulfilment, distribution. Location, access, clear height, and timing decide how well it runs. We make sure they’re right.

From Brief
to Keys.

How it works:
Intake. We map exactly what your operation needs — size, location, access, clear height, timing, and budget.
Search & Select. We scan the market, shortlist the best-fit warehouses, and arrange the viewings.
Secure & Hand Over. We negotiate your lease or purchase, support the documentation, and coordinate handover.
For Occupiers
We Work for You. Not the Landlord.
STAAL represents the occupier — the company that will actually use the space. No divided loyalty, no listing bias. You get one independent, hands-on advisor who knows the Dutch logistics market, negotiates hard on your behalf, and stays with you from the first brief to the day you collect the keys. When a request falls outside our scope, we bring in trusted partners rather than hand you off.
